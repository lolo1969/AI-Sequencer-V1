# ----------------------
# AMBIENT / DRONE
# ----------------------

[AMBIENT_01]
Deep ambient landscape with ethereal pad swells.
Very few notes, maximum space between sounds.
Slowly building like morning mist rising over a still lake.
E Dorian. Inspired by Stars of the Lid and Eluvium.
No sudden changes, everything flows imperceptibly together.

[AMBIENT_02]
Nocturnal drone. Deep, humming textures that shift
almost imperceptibly. Scattered high tones like
distant stars. Very slow, almost stationary.
A minor with Lydian influences.

[AMBIENT_03]
Crystalline ambient meditation. High, bell-like tones
over a warm pad bed. Like sunlight breaking through
ice crystals. C major, but not too cheerful.
Inspired by Harold Budd and Brian Eno.

# MINIMAL / ELECTRONIC
# ----------------------

[MINIMAL_01]

Dotted arpeggios that evolve over long periods.
Mechanical yet organic, like a breathing machine.
124 BPM. Reference: Richie Hawtin, Robert Hood.

[MINIMAL_02]
Microhouse landscape. Gently pulsating, not aggressive.
Small melodic fragments that appear and disappear.
Lots of space, reduced to the essentials.
116 BPM, F minor.

[MINIMAL_03]
Berlin School sequencer music. Analog, warm arpeggios
that slowly shift in pitch.
Cosmic and hypnotic. Tangerine Dream meets modern minimalism.
100 BPM, Dorian in D.

[NEOCLASSICAL_02]

[NEOCLASSICAL_03]
